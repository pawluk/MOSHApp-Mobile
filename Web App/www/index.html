                    MoshApp Help

                    MoshApp is designed to familiarize all George Brown College students with the many different services offered by the college.

                    • Scan QR Codes Scan QR codes to see questions that are related to the current task. Once you have scanned a QR code and answered a question, the time you take to answer the question will be added to your total time. Note: Be careful when answering questions, each wrong answer entered will add 5 minutes to your total time.
                    • Chatting You can use this page in order to chat live with your teammates.
                    • Logging In You will be able to use your GBC username and password to login to MoshApp.
                    • Contacting Team Once you are assigned to a team you will get team member contact information. You can communicate with your team members based on their preferences.
                    • Tasks The task page will allow you to view all the tasks that are available and you can select one to complete.
                      Note: Be careful when choosing a task because you must complete the task in order to select a new task.
                    • Call Facilitator
                    • E-mail Facilitator

                    Login/Logout

                    Logging out: Once you log out you must have internet connection to log in again.

                    Log out
